## Food

Didn't have cooking facilities in Whitehorse, so I explored some restaurants there. But first, Ontario:
* [Markham Station](https://markhamstation.com/): despite the name, actually in Scarborough; competent diner, not crowded on a Friday night.
* [Fratelli Village Pizzeria](https://www.fratellis.ca/): small place; good pasta, also in Scarborough; pizza oven broken when we were there (they warned us)
* [Naan Kabob](https://naankabob.ca/): 10-location chain with good kabob and somehow lots of birthdays on a Sunday
* Banh Mi Saigon, Waterloo: I didn't think my banh mi tasted like anything? Maybe I'll give them another chance.
* [Miga Japanese & Korean](https://migafoodscanada.ca/): Chatted with the owner, who is ethnically South Asian but grew up in Korea and sold a store in PEI. Had a curry. Decent option.
* [Risposta Bistro](https://www.rispostabistro.com/): On the way to Rattlesnake Point; had been to previous restaurant in that location. Fancy Italian place with tasty but fancy food.
* [Ajisan Ramen](https://www.ajisenramen.ca/): Ramen chain with a location in Waterloo. Maybe I should have had ramen, not fried rice.
